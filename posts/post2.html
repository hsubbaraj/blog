My "Serious" Problem With Weight and Trying to Gain It

Food oh Food, You are The Bane of my Existence

Posted by Hari Subbaraj on November 14, 2015

You know that feeling when you try really really hard at something, like I mean put all your effort (or in my case, about a quarter of the effort I am capable of), and you still fail miserably? It truly is a horrible feeling. It’s the feeling I get after a multivariable test, or what the Dolphins feel like after getting crushed by the Pats. I digress, lets get back to the point. That feeling of helplessness is a somewhat common event in my life, but there is one time that it can never fail to well up in my mind- when I hit the scales.

My friends describe me as a pretty thin guy. Never mind that’s complete bullshit. The only reason they don’t crack skeleton jokes all the time is because one of my best friends is skinnier than I am, looking at you Nihal. I weight about 123 lbs., or for those smart non ‘Murican people who use the metric system, 55.79 kg. That may seem like a decent weight, but for a guy that’s 6’1’’, well let’s just say that it’s very hard to find me on that BMI chart. The weird thing is, I don’t see myself as overly skinny. I am relatively fit (or I like to think so) and I eat a lot, it’s just that I simply cannot gain weight. I know at this point that you are super skeptical, I mean, just eat a lot of junk food and you’ll start to balloon. The problem is, I’m just not a junk food guy, and it’s not because of my parents either. In fact, when my parents were out of the country for 10 days, I could’ve eaten anything and everything. Instead, I ended up finishing all the fruits in my house, along with the occasional chapathi and curry. The two times I went out to eat, I got a salad and chocolate milk from Safeway. Unfortunately, that pigging out gained me all of -3 pounds.

To find the reason of why I couldn’t gain weight, I tried to analyze why exactly I wasn’t making any gains. After several hours of careful introspection, I realized that I really do not like eating. The though of putting stuff in my mouth, swallowing, and eventually shitting it out just doesn’t really really sound like an amazing thing to me. Yea, some food tastes great, but I don’t eat for taste. I really only eat when I am hungry, and while I do appreciate good food, I am not overly picky when I eat, which is crazy, cause my mom is a chef of the highest caliber. I guess I like being skinny, but I definitely want to make some changes. But as the saying goes, life is always greener on the other side

And so, I have made a life-changing decision. I am going to try my hardest to become fat. Why eat celery sticks when you can have French fries? I’ll toss that bowl of grapes for that oh so buttery popcorn. You name it, I will eat it. To start off, I asked my mom to buy a box of Ferrero Rocher chocolates, and after consuming about half the box in two days, I have to say, this junk food thing may be growing on me.

Note: this is probably the worst possible way for me to gain weight. I should be hitting that gym and getting swole, but I am kinda lazy haha.
