Time to Give Thanks

Posted by Hari Subbaraj on November 26, 2015

It is November 26, also known as the day before Black Friday. All jokes aside (apparently it is now Black Thursday/Friday/The whole fucking weekend now), it is Thanksgiving, or the day we Americans celebrate the good natured relationship between Native Americans and the mass murdering European settlers. I have no idea how this relationship of all things should be deserving of thanks, but I do really like the idea of thanking all the important people in my life, so here it is folks: Hari’s Thanks Giving!!!

I want to thank my parents first and foremost, for putting up with all the shit that I do, and for always loving me unconditionally. You are amazing people, and I will always love you till the end of my days.

I want to thank my sisters for being amazing role models for me, and although you kinda have fucked me over in terms of expectations, I will always be proud of your numerous accomplishments and wish you both the best of luck for your future. God, I really hope you don’t read this.

So now that the familial thanks are done, lets move on to the fun ones (rubs hands together)

I want to thank my three best friends (you know who you are), always the source of moral righteousness and good advice. I can’t imagine where myself without the absolutely wonderful influences you guys have been on me. Oh shit, I actually can…. So that concludes my Thanks Giving. Please forgive me if I have not included you in the list above, I love you just as much, I just couldn’t think of any description that wouldn’t disappoint you. So thanks once again to everybody, and I hope you all had a wonderful Thanksgiving!
