About Me

This is what I do.

My name is Hari Subbaraj. I am 17 years old and live in Fremont, CA. I attend Mission San Jose High School.

I am the third and youngest child of my family, born to immigrant parents and brought up in perhaps the most unique community in all of America. I have a passion for computer science(check out my website here) and engineering, which I definitely hope to pursue in the future. I also enjoy writing, although this is the first time I am actually letting anyone other than my poor English teachers read it. This unfortunately sounds too much like a college application, but I think knowing some background about me is important context for the posts on this blog.

All of that is nice and pretty, but who am I? It is a question that I myself don't have an answer to, as my underwhelming college essays can attest to. In fact, that is precisely why I am writing this blog, to document my thoughts and feelings in an attempt to discover more about myself. It may sound very cheesy and cliche, but this venture is something very important and new to me. So buckle up, and get ready to explore the depths(hopefully they exist) of my mind.
